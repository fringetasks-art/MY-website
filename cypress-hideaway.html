Our Story

About Us

Why we built Cypress Hideaway — and what drives us every day.

Built for the Seekers

Cypress Hideaway was built on a deliberate decision: stay small. With only 15 sites, we made a choice that most campgrounds won't — to keep the land more wild than developed, more quiet than busy, more nature than noise.

That decision changes everything. Because we have fewer guests, the deer still graze near the sites at dawn. The barred owls still call at dusk. The Milky Way shows up on clear nights without a crowd of flashlights blocking the view. The silence between campsites is real silence — not just distance between RVs.

We're located in the heart of North-Central Florida, chosen for its ancient cypress trees, dark skies, and the kind of stillness that city life makes you forget was possible. We refuse to grow beyond 15 sites — not because we can't, but because doing so would destroy exactly what makes this place worth coming to.

Policies & Rules

Policies

Please read carefully before booking.

🏡 House Rules
Cypress Hideaway is designed as a peaceful retreat for all guests. By booking, you agree to respect these rules for the enjoyment of all.
  • No parties, large gatherings, or events of any kind
  • Quiet hours are strictly enforced: 10:00 PM – 8:00 AM daily
  • Generators are prohibited at all times
  • No loud music or amplified sound at any time
  • All guests must be registered at check-in — no unregistered visitors allowed on property
  • Respect other guests' privacy and space
  • Do not enter any site other than your own
  • Fires are permitted in designated fire rings only — never leave unattended
  • Do not cut or damage trees, vegetation, or property
  • Occupancy limits per site must not be exceeded
  • No weapons permitted on property
  • Management reserves the right to remove guests without refund for violations
🐾 Pet Policy
We welcome well-behaved pets with advance notice. Please inform us of your pet(s) at booking.
  • Maximum 2 pets per site (additional approval required)
  • Pets must remain on a leash (6 ft max) at all times when outside your tent or vehicle
  • Pets are not permitted in the bathhouse or any enclosed common areas
  • You are responsible for cleaning up after your pet immediately
  • Pets must not be left unattended on-site
  • Excessive barking or aggressive behavior will result in immediate removal
📅 Cancellation Policy
We understand plans change. Our cancellation policy is designed to be fair while protecting the availability of our limited sites.
  • More than 14 days before arrival: Full refund minus a $25 processing fee
  • 7–14 days before arrival: 50% refund of total reservation cost
  • Less than 7 days before arrival: No refund
  • Modifications to dates are subject to availability and may incur fees
  • No-shows are charged in full
🌧️ Weather Policy
Florida weather is part of the outdoor experience. We do not issue refunds due to weather, with one exception.
Named Hurricane Exception: If a named hurricane is forecast to make landfall within 75 miles of our property within 72 hours of your check-in date, we will issue a full credit valid for 12 months. No cash refunds are provided for weather events under any other circumstances.
  • Rain, thunderstorms, and extreme heat are not grounds for refund or cancellation
  • We recommend travel insurance for additional coverage
  • Cold snaps, wind, or other weather events are not refundable
⚠️ Liability Disclaimer
Cypress Hideaway Glamping & Retreat operates in a natural outdoor environment. By booking and entering the property, guests acknowledge and accept the following:
  • Guests participate in all activities at their own risk
  • Cypress Hideaway is not responsible for injuries, illness, or accidents occurring on the property
  • Wildlife encounters (insects, reptiles, mammals) are inherent to outdoor environments — we are not liable for wildlife-related incidents
  • Guests are responsible for their own safety, belongings, and the behavior of all members of their party including children and pets
  • Any damage to property caused by guests will be billed to the card on file
  • Cypress Hideaway is not responsible for loss or theft of personal belongings
